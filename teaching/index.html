Teaching Experience


Associate Lecturer at Aberystwyth University

In July 2023, I was given a part-time Associate Lecturer position at Aberystwyth University. My role was primarily translating various documents (lecture material, assignments, exam papers, etc.) from English to Welsh, alongside my duties as a postgraduate demonstrator.

Postgraduate Demonstrator at Aberystwyth University

During the course of my PhD, I was a Postgraduate Demonstrator for the following undergraduate modules:

  • PH05720 - Introduction to Laboratory Physics
  • PH15720 - Laboratory Techniques for Experimental Physics
  • PH10020 - Dynamics, Waves and Heat


Associate Fellow of the Higher Education Academy

In 2023, I successfully completed the Teaching for Postgraduates at Aberystwyth University (TPAU) scheme, a highly competitive programme accredited through the Higher Education Academy (HEA) at Associate Fellowship level. It required a minimum of 20 hours of teaching and 12 hours of CPD during the year and was assessed through a combination of reflective essays and peer observations of my teaching.
